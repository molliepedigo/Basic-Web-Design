Storyline

As Harry returns for his third year at Hogwarts, he faces more than just his studies. The escape of Sirius Black from the infamous Azkaban prison sends the wizarding world into a panic. But as Harry uncovers new truths about his parents, his world is changed forever.

Behind the Scenes

Directorial Shift: "With Alfonso Cuarón taking over the director’s chair, Prisoner of Azkaban marked a darker, more mature turn in the Harry Potter series. His vision introduced unique visual styles and complex emotional depth that enhanced the storyline."
