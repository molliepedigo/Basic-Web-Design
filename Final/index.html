Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a series of novels by British author J. K. Rowling. The novels follow Harry Potter, an 11-year-old boy who discovers he is the son of famous wizards and will attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Harry learns of an entire society of wizards and witches. He befriends several Hogwarts students and teachers along his journey. Each novel chronicles one year in Harry's life and his adventures discovering the events that led to his parents' deaths at the hands of Lord Voldermort, a dark wizard believed long dead.
