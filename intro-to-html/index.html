Suits

Set in a fictional New York City corporate law firm, the series follows Mike Ross (Patrick J. Adams), a college dropout with a photographic memory, as he works as an associate for the successful and charismatic attorney, Harvey Specter (Gabriel Macht).

Characters

There are a lot of charaters in suits, but the main ones are, Mike Ross, Harvey Specter, Jessica Pearson, Donna Paulsen, Rachel Zane, and Louis Litt.

Random Facts

  • The series was inspired by the creators own life
  • There is a spinoff
  • One of the actors married into the British Royal family
