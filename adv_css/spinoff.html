Suits Spinoff: Pearson

Pearson

Pearson is a legal and political drama series that serves as a spin-off of the popular legal drama Suits. It focuses on the character Jessica Pearson, portrayed by Gina Torres, who was a major figure in Suits. Pearson premiered on July 17, 2019, on the USA Network.

Plot

The show centers around Jessica Pearson, a former managing partner of Pearson Specter Litt (the fictional law firm from Suits), as she makes a career shift into the world of Chicago politics. After being disbarred from practicing law, Jessica accepts a job working for Bobby Novak, the Mayor of Chicago, and soon finds herself entangled in the murky world of political corruption. She struggles to balance her ethics, power dynamics, and her moral compass as she navigates the cutthroat world of politics.

Suits Fans

If you're a fan of Suits and interested in political dramas, Pearson offers a new angle on the world Jessica Pearson inhabits post-law career.
